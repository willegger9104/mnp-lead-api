How to Structure Your First Sale

The Conversation

  • Demo Sarah live on the call
  • Show dashboard with real leads
  • State price confidently: "$1,000 setup, $250/month"
  • Ask: "Want me to get this live by Friday?"
  • If yes → send invoice same day
  • Collect setup fee before starting work

After They Say Yes

  • Send service agreement (get signed)
  • Collect $1,000 setup via Venmo/Zelle
  • Clone your MnP codebase
  • Swap company name, phone, colors
  • Configure Vapi with their info
  • Go live — send dashboard link
