This Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of the date signed below between Will Egger ("Provider") and the Client named above. Both parties agree to the following terms:

1. Services Provided

Provider will design, build, configure, and maintain an AI-powered voice agent system ("Sarah") including:

  • Custom AI voice agent configured for Client's business
  • Lead intake and call handling (24/7)
  • Emergency call routing to Client's designated emergency line
  • Live lead dashboard accessible via web browser
  • Ongoing maintenance and script updates as requested
  • Monthly performance summary

2. Fees & Payment

One-Time Setup Fee: $1,000 — due before work begins.
Monthly Retainer: $250/month — due on the 1st of each month.

Payment accepted via Venmo, Zelle, check, or bank transfer. Late payments over 7 days may result in service suspension. Setup fee is non-refundable once work has commenced.

3. Term & Cancellation

This agreement begins on the date signed and continues month-to-month. Either party may cancel with 14 days written notice via email. No long-term commitment required. Upon cancellation, the Provider will ensure a clean handoff of any data or dashboard access.

4. Client Responsibilities

  • Provide accurate business information (name, address, phone, hours)
  • Designate an emergency contact number for call routing
  • Notify Provider of any changes to business info or call handling preferences
  • Respond to Provider within 48 hours for setup-related questions

5. Data & Privacy

All lead data collected belongs to the Client. Provider will not share, sell, or distribute Client data to any third party. Data is stored securely in a cloud database. Client may request a full export or deletion of their data at any time.

6. Limitations of Liability

Provider is not liable for missed calls resulting from third-party platform outages (Vapi, Supabase, Render), internet disruptions, or circumstances outside Provider's control. Provider will make reasonable efforts to resolve outages promptly. The AI voice agent is a supplemental tool and does not replace legal, emergency, or medical services.

7. Intellectual Property

The Provider retains ownership of the underlying system architecture, code, and AI configuration. The Client owns their business data and branding customizations. Provider may reference Client as a case study unless Client requests otherwise in writing.

8. Modifications

Either party may request modifications to service scope. Additional feature development beyond the agreed scope may incur additional fees, agreed upon in writing before work begins.

9. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Colorado. Any disputes will be resolved through good-faith negotiation before pursuing legal remedies.
